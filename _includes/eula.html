  1. Payments

10.1  The Licensor will be responsible for issuing the charge type in accordance with Clover standards in four different sources: Subscription, Prorated Subscription, Partial Month, and Metered.

(a) Subscription – a monthly subscription rate of zero or more will generate a Subscription charge for that amount at the beginning of every month for each merchant (MID) who already has that app installed. Unlike the other charge types, Subscription charges are payments for the current/upcoming month. So on January first, Subscription charges will be generated for the subscription period of the month of January. Subscription  charges will only ever be generated at the beginning of full month periods.

(b) Prorated Subscription -any upgrades or downgrades the subscription part way through a month, and the effective monthly subscription rate changes, a Prorated Subscription charge will be generated immediately at the time of the upgrades or downgrades. If moving to a higher monthly subscription rate, the Prorated Subscription  will be a charge for the difference in rate for the remainder of the month at the time of the change. If moving to a lower monthly subscription rate, the Prorated Subscription  will be a refund for the difference in the rate for the remainder of the month at the time of the change.

(c) Partial Month – If it is not precisely the beginning of a billing period when the end of a free trial period with an app (or installs an app without a free trial), a Partial Month charge will be generated on the day the billable subscription rate activates. This charge will be a partial monthly subscription amount for the remainder of the month long billing period after the app first becomes billable.

(d) Metered – a metered event is triggered for an app with billable metered events, Clover collects the event. On the first of every month all of the metered events a merchant generated for a given app during the previous month are totaled together into a single Metered charge.
