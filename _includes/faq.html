FAQ

How do I install Self Serve Kiosk?

  1. Download Self Serve Kiosk via the Clover App Market
  2. Contact Us via this form so we can create your Kiosk Management Portal and App Login.
  3. Logon to the your Clover POS hardware and enter your login infomation to activate Customer Facing Mode.

How do I manage my Photos and Descriptions?

  1. Login to your Kiosk Management Portal. You will see a list of your Items and Modifiers
  2. Click on the Item or Modifier you want to change an d you will be able to edit the photos and description of your chosen item..
  3. Press Save.

How do I exit Kiosk Mode?

Press all four corners of the screen and enter the pin code 6369.

How does Self Service Kiosk Handle Payments?

All payments are made through the Clover Register App.
